About

Technical depth matters. So does judgment.

I like working where the input is ambiguous, the workflow is messy, and the right product has to create structure rather than decorate chaos.

My work tends to live between modeling, systems design, and product strategy. I am most interested in AI that earns its place inside real operations, where reliability, state, and decision quality matter more than demo value. That lens runs through Chartera, through the multimodal and agentic systems I care about, and through the broader questions I keep returning to around institutions, coordination, and leverage.
